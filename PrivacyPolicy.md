# Privacy Policy
## Effective Date: 2026-06-10
This Privacy Policy explains how Hermes-bot ("we," "us," or "our") handles your information when you interact with our Discord bot. Protecting your privacy is a core principle of our design.
## 1. What Information We Collect
We do not collect, store, or log any personal information.
When you use the bot, your commands and text prompts are sent directly to our connected Artificial Intelligence (AI) agent to generate a response.
Your message content is processed ephemerally (in real-time) and is never saved to a database, hard drive, or any external storage.
## 2. How We Use Information
Because we do not collect or store your information, we do not use your data for any purpose, including marketing, analytics, or AI model training.
## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure
Since we do not retain your data, we do not share, sell, or disclose your personal information to any third parties. We are not responsible for how Discord handles your data; please refer to the Discord Privacy Policy for more details.
## 4. Children's Privacy
Hermes-bot is not directed to individuals under the age of 18 (or the relevant age of digital consent in your region). Because we do not collect any user data, we do not knowingly interact with children.
## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our bot's functionality. We will announce any changes in the server where the bot is hosted.
